MYSTIC NIGHTS DANCE WORKSHOPS

June 30, 2017 | KBCE

We are aiming to bring you the latest dance choreographies from all the hottest groups like EXO and BLACKPINK.

Mystic Nights are a co-ed group of dancers that all specialize in unique styles. We stay on top of all the Hallyu News and work alongside companies to share this segment of Korean culture in a fun and engaging way.

We will be teaching via request and popularity. With classes open to all ages and levels, we are here to make all the choreographies as easy and fun to learn as we can in a community filled with fellow kpoppers that you can share the experience with.

We are starting classes off small and spaces will be limited so it is best that you get back to us as soon as possible to grab a space. Be sure to share the news as we are excited to meet you!
