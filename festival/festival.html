KINGSTON KOREAN FESTIVAL

Following the great success of last year’s Autumn Full Moon Festival, Kingston Korean Festival 2017 returns to Kingston where the biggest Korean community in Europe is based. This year, Kingston Korean Festival runs two main programmes to celebrate Korean culture and to engage with local people in many ways.  Experience how Koreans are celebrating its first birthday at the Kingston Museum. Come and enjoy community Fest at the market place! Try Korean food and watch performances, such K-Dances, K-Music and Hapkido featuring by Koreans and Locals artists uniting together to give an outstanding show.
Kingston Korean Festival presents something new, unexpected and exciting community event that Korean community would like to welcome everyone to experience Korean culture so we can better understand each other and build a better Kingston together. There will be fundraising activities and events to support local causes. 

Exhibition | Kingston Museum, Kingston

‘Dol’, Baby's First Birthday Celebration in Korea 
31st August ~ 3rd October 2017

Community Fest | Ancient Market Place, Kingston

12:00~16:00, Sat 16th  September 2017

Kingston Korean Festival welcomes everyone to celebrate and experience Korean culture! Enjoy fun and inclusive arts activities for free!
This year, the festival runs two family oriented programmes, an Exhibition and a Community Fest which are something new, unexpected, exciting and authentic!
It will be a great opportunity for everyone to learn from each other to build a better Kingston. There will be fundraising activities along with the festival to support the local causes.

The Kingston Korean Festival is an annual festival of celebrating vibrant Korean culture in Kingston and providing some of the most varied entertainment and educational programme for everyone.
