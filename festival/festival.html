KINGSTON KOREAN FESTIVAL

Following the great success of last year’s Autumn Full Moon Festival, Kingston Korean Festival 2017 returns to Kingston where the biggest Korean community in Europe is based. This year, Kingston Korean Festival runs two main programmes to celebrate Korean culture and engage with british people in many ways. Experience how Koreans are celebrate there first birthday at the Kingston Museum. Come and enjoy community Fest at the market place! Try mouth-watering Korean food and watch various performances, such K-Dances, K-Music and Hapkido created by Koreans artists in collaboration with the local British artists.

Kingston Korean Festival will be a unique and exciting community event and Korean community would like to welcome everyone to take part and experience Korean culture. There will be also fundraising activities and events to support local causes. 


Exhibition | ‘Dol’, Baby's First Birthday Celebration in Korea

31st August ~ 3rd October 2017 
at Kingston Museum, Kingston

Community Fest

12:00~16:00, Sat 16th  September 2017
